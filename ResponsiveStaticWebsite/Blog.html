Why Codoid ?

With years of experience and knowledge gathering, we are equipped to manage even the most stringent demands of discerning clients. Our customers stay with us and recommend our QA services, which is a testament to the expertise of our QA team and the superiority and accuracy of our testing methods. We advise our clients on their QA strategy and provide them with invaluable information on the right test approach for their business and products. This is the collection of the best projects we have worked on.

Beginning as a startup in 2012, Codoid has rapidly established itself as one of the TOP QA COMPANIES in the world. Codoid is proudly led by QA experts that are passionate about software testing, and Quality Assurance. The greater the challenge, the more driven our team becomes. We don’t stop until we’ve achieved our goal of delivering quality software. Our motivation comes from a deep need to learn, explore, and grow—as a team. As we delve into the quality assessment of your product, we leave absolutely no stone unturned in ensuring your product meets the needs of the end user. Our team is focused on meeting the highest standards, to help you deploy your incredible work. Based in the tech hubs of India and Singapore, our clients are global. We work with organizations from fast-growing startups, to Fortune 500 companies. All of our clients trust Codoid to deliver incredible QA testing, everytime.
